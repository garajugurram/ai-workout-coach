# User Personas

---

## Persona 1 — The Self-Starter
**Marcus, 23 | College Senior | Lagos**

**Goal:** Build muscle and look better before graduation.
**Current behavior:** Follows random YouTube programs, switches every 3 weeks when progress stalls, makes up workouts on days he can't find a video.
**Pain point:** No structure. No idea if he's progressing or just moving weights around.
**Budget:** $0 for coaching. Pays for Spotify but nothing fitness-specific.
**What he needs:** A plan that tells him exactly what to do each week and gets harder as he gets stronger.
**Relationship with tech:** High. On his phone constantly. Wants something fast to set up.

> *"I don't need a trainer. I just need to know what I'm supposed to do tomorrow."*

---

## Persona 2 — The Time-Strapped Professional
**Priya, 34 | Product Manager | London**

**Goal:** Stay consistent, maintain fitness, manage stress.
**Current behavior:** Trains 3x per week when work allows. Has tried Peloton, Nike Training Club, cancelled both. Knows what she's doing in the gym but loses momentum when life gets busy.
**Pain point:** No accountability. No one adjusts her plan when she misses a day or has a brutal week at work.
**Budget:** Would pay $10/month but won't commit until she's seen value.
**What she needs:** Something that adapts to her actual week, not an ideal one. Playlists she doesn't have to think about.
**Relationship with tech:** Medium-high. Wants minimal friction, hates onboarding.

> *"I just want to open the app, know what I'm doing, press play, and go."*

---

## Persona 3 — The Comeback Adult
**David, 47 | Secondary School Teacher | Manchester**

**Goal:** Lose weight, rebuild strength after a 5-year gap from training.
**Current behavior:** Recently rejoined a gym. Watches form videos on YouTube before each exercise. Nervous about injury. Doesn't know where to start with a program.
**Pain point:** Intimidated by complex programs. Worried about doing exercises wrong. Can't justify paying a trainer on a teacher's salary.
**Budget:** Free only for now. Might pay $5–8/month if he sticks with it past 3 months.
**What he needs:** Simple plan, clear form feedback, recovery guidance his body actually needs at 47.
**Relationship with tech:** Medium. Comfortable with apps but doesn't want to learn a new system.

> *"I just don't want to hurt myself doing it wrong. And I need someone to tell me I'm on the right track."*

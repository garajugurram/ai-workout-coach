# AI Workout Coach
### AI-Powered Coaching for People Who Can't Afford a Coach

---

## The Problem
A personal trainer costs $60–150 per session. Most people who want structured, adaptive training are left choosing between generic YouTube videos and expensive human coaching. There is nothing in between.

## Who It's For
Self-motivated fitness enthusiasts (21–60) who have gym access, know they should be training smarter, but lack the programming knowledge and real-time feedback to do it alone.

## What It Does
AI Workout Coach delivers a full week of personalized training every Monday — adaptive plan, matched playlist, and a 60-second audio briefing — then learns from how each session actually went. After every workout, users record a quick voice note. The AI adjusts next week's intensity automatically. Users can also upload exercise videos for AI form feedback with a safety score and coaching cues.

## Core Features
- **Adaptive Weekly Plan** — built from a 5-question onboarding, rebuilt every week from voice feedback
- **Audio Briefing** — 60-second Monday morning summary of the week ahead
- **Energy-Matched Playlists** — YouTube playlists selected by workout type, not generic mood
- **Voice Note Adaptation** — natural language session feedback that shifts next week's intensity
- **AI Form Check** — score + text feedback on uploaded squat or calf raise video, with safety disclaimer
- **Recovery Cards** — post-workout stretching tips after every session

## Why This Wins
Voice note adaptation removes the friction of every other coaching app (no RPE sliders, no manual logging). Playlist + plan + form check in one place replaces three separate tools. The combination is the moat — not any single feature.

## Build Plan
**Phase 1** — Plan generation, voice adaptation, audio briefing, recovery cards
**Phase 2** — YouTube playlist integration
**Phase 3** — AI form check (squat + calf raise)

## Business Model
Free at launch to drive adoption. Subscription at $8–12/month introduced once weekly retention is proven. Premium gate: form check history, advanced customisation, expanded exercise library.

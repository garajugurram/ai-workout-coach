# Landing Page Design Prompt — AI Workout Coach

**Design a modern, dark-mode mobile-first landing page for an AI fitness coaching app called "AI Workout Coach."**

---

## Brand Direction
- Tone: Confident, no-nonsense, accessible. Not corporate. Not intimidating. Feels like a smart friend who trains.
- Aesthetic: Dark background (#0D0D0D or #111111), electric accent color (neon green #B8FF4F or electric blue #4FFFB0), clean white body text.
- Typography: Bold condensed display font for headlines (e.g. Bebas Neue or Barlow Condensed), clean sans-serif for body (Inter or DM Sans).
- Imagery style: Real people training — not stock photo smiles. Candid gym shots, motion blur, gritty but clean.

---

## Page Sections (top to bottom)

**1. Navigation**
Logo left. "Sign Up Free" CTA button right. Minimal — no more than 3 nav links.

**2. Hero**
- Headline (large, bold): *"Your coach. Zero cost."*
- Subheadline: *"Adaptive weekly plans, AI form feedback, and energy-matched playlists — built for people who train without a trainer."*
- Primary CTA button: "Get My Free Plan"
- Secondary link: "See how it works ↓"
- Right side: phone mockup showing the weekly plan screen inside the app

**3. Problem Strip**
3 pain points in a horizontal row with icons:
- "Generic plans that don't adapt"
- "No feedback on your form"
- "Trainers you can't afford"

**4. How It Works**
3-step horizontal flow with icons:
1. Answer 5 questions → get your plan
2. Train → record a voice note after each session
3. App adapts next week automatically

**5. Features Grid**
6 cards in a 2x3 grid:
- Adaptive Weekly Plan
- 60-sec Audio Briefing
- Energy-Matched Playlists
- Voice Note Adaptation
- AI Form Check (score + feedback)
- Recovery Cards

Each card: icon + feature name + one-line description.

**6. Form Check Feature Spotlight**
Full-width dark section. Left: phone mockup showing form score screen. Right: headline *"Know if you're doing it right."* + bullet points of what the AI checks. Small disclaimer text at bottom.

**7. Persona Social Proof**
3 cards — one per persona (Marcus, Priya, David). Name, age, short quote. Avatar placeholder. Subtle star rating.

**8. Pricing Strip**
Single card. Badge: "Free Forever to Start". List of what's free. Mention future premium tier without details. CTA: "Start Free — No Credit Card."

**9. Footer**
Logo, tagline, links: Privacy Policy, Terms, Contact.

---

## UI Details
- Rounded corners (border-radius: 16px on cards)
- Subtle gradient overlays on section backgrounds
- Phone mockups: use device frame (iPhone 15 style)
- Animations: fade-in on scroll for each section
- Mobile breakpoint: single column, stacked layout

---

## Do Not Include
- Stock photo gym models smiling at cameras
- Complicated pricing tables
- Testimonials with fake names and 5 stars
- Any mention of competitors

---

## Usage
Paste this prompt into **v0.dev** for a React/Tailwind build, or **Lovable/Bolt** for a full deployable prototype.
